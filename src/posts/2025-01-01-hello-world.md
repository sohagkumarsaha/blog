Welcome to my academic blog. This space is where I collect research notes, project updates, teaching reflections, and small technical examples that may be useful to students and collaborators.

I want this site to feel like a clean notebook: **focused**, *readable*, and easy to maintain. The best research writing often begins as a small note that becomes clearer through revision.

You can use this paragraph to link to an external resource such as [Eleventy](https://www.11ty.dev/) or to an internal page such as the [Academic Profile]({{ '/pages/academic-profile/' | url }}).

> A good research blog does not need to publish perfect ideas. It needs to publish useful thinking in a form that can be revisited, tested, and improved.

Future posts will include code snippets, conference galleries, project demos, and short explanations of computational methods.
